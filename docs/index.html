An innovative application that brings the piano experience to virtual environment using computer vision and artificial intelligence-based hand detection technology. The application detects hand and finger positions in real-time through the camera, analyzing fingertip coordinates to map them to predefined virtual keys. Our Working Process

From Discovery to Deployment:
Our Process

01
Consultation

We begin with an in-depth consultation to understand your business goals, objectives, and target audience. This initial discussion allows us to align our strategies with your vision and ensure our project are perfectly tailored to your needs.

02
Research and Strategy Development

Following the consultation, our team conducts thorough market research and competitor analysis to identify key opportunities. We then develop a comprehensive, data-driven strategy designed to achieve your specific objectives effectively.

03
Technology Validation

We assess your current technology stack and validate the tools required for successful implementation. This step ensures that all technical systems are correctly configured and optimized to support the strategy.

04
MVP Implementation

We build and launch a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) that includes the core functionalities essential for an early market entry. This provides a stable foundation to build upon and gather initial user interactions.

05
Iterative Development & Enhancement

With the MVP successfully launched, we move into an iterative development cycle. Based on the strategic roadmap, we systematically build and deploy additional features in planned phases, transforming the core product into a more comprehensive and robust solution.

06
Monitoring and Optimization

As the product evolves with new features, we continuously monitor the performance of the entire system against key metrics. We analyze user behavior and data to make ongoing optimizations that improve usability, engagement, and overall results.

07
Reporting and Communication

We believe in full transparency, providing you with regular, easy-to-understand reports that highlight development progress, performance metrics, and key insights. Our team maintains open communication to keep you informed every step of the way.
